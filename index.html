About

My favorite hobby is basketball. I have been playing basketball for about 5 years now in a league and have enjoyed it for all my life. I play a travel ball league and the position I play is point guard. This position allows you to bring the ball up the court and pass, shoot, drive, fake, etc. I like basketball beacuse of the quickness of the game and the ability you have to do anything you want and make situations happen in the game, especially in the position I play

My favorite nba player is Stephen Curry. I like Curry because of his ability to shoot the ball and it inspires me because I am a shooter too. I am small and quick like him so he inspires me and teaches me in his games because of how similar we are in size. Basketball has been my #1 sport my whole life although I have taken interest in things like baseball and soccer both in which I played for a short amount of time. I encourage people to play basketball because they won't regret it once they start.
